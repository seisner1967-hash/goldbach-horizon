\begin{abstract}
This document is the arXiv-friendly final attestation layer of the Horizon Programme for the asymptotic Goldbach theorem. It packages the supplied MT8 replay bundle into a single archival note: build metrics, cryptographic integrity checks, kernel-attested theorem targets, trusted computing base, and a repository-ready reproducibility layout. The attestation status recorded here is \emph{compiled-and-attested}: the machine-readable summary reports zero errors, zero warnings, zero \texttt{sorry} terms, zero nonstandard axioms, four attested theorem targets, and SHA-256 integrity checks for the frozen MT6--MT8 artifacts. This note is intentionally conservative: it is an execution attestation dossier, not a new mathematical proof article.
\end{abstract}

\section{Position of FSN v9 in the MT chain}
The role of FSN v9 is not to introduce new mathematics. Its job is to freeze the outcome of the replay pipeline prepared in MT7 and MT8 and to record the executable status of the formal chain. In the final attested bundle, the following theorem targets are marked as present:
\begin{enumerate}[label=(\alph*)]
    \item \texttt{Horizon.Goldbach.A2\_KLMN\_closed},
    \item \texttt{Horizon.Goldbach.MT4\_F1\_of\_A2},
    \item \texttt{Horizon.Goldbach.MT3\_goldbach\_from\_F1},
    \item \texttt{Horizon.Goldbach.goldbach\_asymptotic\_theorem}.
\end{enumerate}
Together with the frozen witness hash for \texttt{A2\_certified\_v3.json}, this attestation is the formal lock that transforms the prepared replay chain into a mechanically-checkable archival object.

\section{Trusted computing base}
The final TCB is intentionally narrow and explicit:
\begin{enumerate}[label=\arabic*.]
    \item the Lean 4.29.0 kernel,
    \item the GMP/MPFR arithmetic stack used by the trusted runtime,
    \item the uploaded MT6 witness file at parse time, checked by SHA-256,
    \item the execution environment (operating system and hardware).
\end{enumerate}
No nonstandard axioms and no incomplete proof terms are recorded in the attested metrics bundle.
